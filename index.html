About the Event

This website is so that people who wish to donate to certain causes or charities can do so without worrying about whether or not the thing they are donating to is legitimate.

There are many people who wish to donate but do not know if they will be able to donate their money safely, securly, and legitimately. This website is to help people who have those worries and to placate those worries with links to websites and donations that have been confirmed legitimate.
